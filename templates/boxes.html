Appendix:

  Modifier Class Assign To Details
.box n/a Applies 1rem / 16px of padding around content.
.box--x2 n/a Applies 2rem / 32px of padding around content.
.box--x3 n/a Applies 3rem / 48px of padding around content.
.box--light .box Applies a white background color behind content.
.box--dusty .box Applies a grey (dusty) background color behind content.
.box--framed .box Applies a border around content.
.box--rounded .box Applies a 4px border radius.
