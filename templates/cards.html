Appendix:

  Modifier Class Assign To Details
.card * Applies default card style.
.card--flat .card Removes default border-radius from card.
.card--framed .card Applies secondary 1px border style to card.
.card--shadow .card Applies drop shadow to card.
.card__header * Applies default card header style.
.card__header--dusty .card__header Applies dusty background color to card header.
.card__body * Applies default card body style with 16px padding.
.card__body--flush .card__body Removes default 16px padding from card body.
.card__body--bordered .card__body Applies top and bottom borders to card body.
.card__footer * Applies default card footer style.
.card__footer--dusty .card__footer Applies dusty background color to card footer.
