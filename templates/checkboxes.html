  Modifier Class Assign To Details
  .option * Manages formatting of checkbox and associated label.
  .option__input <input type="checkbox" / > Hides default checkbox.
  .option__icon * Applies styling to element to have checkbox unchecked/checked state.
  .option__text * Applies styling to element to have checkbox label text.
  .option--hasError .option Applies error styling to the checkbox.
  .option--disabled .option Applies disabled styling to the checkbox.
